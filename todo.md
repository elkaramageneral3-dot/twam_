# الكرامة جينيرال - صفحة هبوط TWAM

## Design Guidelines

### Design References
- **Luxury confectionery brands**: Dark, rich backgrounds with golden accents
- **Style**: Dark Luxury + Golden Elegance + Arabic Heritage

### Color Palette
- Primary Background: #0A0A0A (Deep Black)
- Secondary Background: #1A1410 (Dark Chocolate Brown)
- Accent Gold: #D4A853 (Rich Gold)
- Accent Orange: #E8792B (Brand Orange from logo)
- Warm Amber: #C4883A (Warm Gold)
- Text Primary: #FFFFFF (White)
- Text Secondary: #B8A080 (Warm Beige)
- Card Background: #1C1612 (Dark Brown)

### Typography
- Headings Arabic: Noto Kufi Arabic, font-weight 700
- Body Arabic: Noto Sans Arabic, font-weight 400
- English/Brand: Playfair Display, font-weight 700
- Body English: Inter, font-weight 400

### Key Component Styles
- Buttons: Gold gradient background, dark text, 8px rounded, hover glow effect
- Cards: Dark brown with gold border on hover, 16px rounded
- Sections: Full-width with parallax-like scroll effects
- Navigation: Transparent to dark on scroll
